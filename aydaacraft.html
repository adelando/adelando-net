How to play

Tutorial

1.Select your download

2.unzip your download and select the worlds you wish to play in.

3.Drag and Drop the world into your Minecraft saves folder in .Minecraft

4.Open your Minecraft launcher

5.Change your version profile (or create a new one) that matches the version you downloaded

6.Launch Minecraft and go to singleplayer

7.Find your downloaded world from your list

8.Select Edit and then look for gamerules

9.set false dofiretick & dotntdamage

10.Once confirmed gamerules are selected, Press play!

11.All done
